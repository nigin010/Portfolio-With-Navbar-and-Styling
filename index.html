Contactless Smart Payment System In Public transport

The ticketing system in most of the public transport vehicles in our country is based on giving money to the bus conductors in-hand. This can lead to many different problems like spreading of germs through money, customers not having the right amount of money to pay for the ticket, conductors not giving back the balance amount and so on. This project mainly focuses on automating the whole ticket taking system in such a way that is very well practical for our country which would solve the ticketing issue as well as reduce the number of conductors there by save some money of the government which is usually given as salary to the conductrs. The main technologies used includes Python, RFID Sensors, RFID Cards, OpenStreet API and Firebase for database.
